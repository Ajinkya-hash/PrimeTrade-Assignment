# Executive Summary

Objective:
Evaluate the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and Hyperliquid trader performance.

Methodology:
- Merged Fear & Greed Index with 211k+ trades.
- Computed trader and daily metrics.
- Evaluated profitability across sentiment states.
- Built trader segments and clusters.
- Performed significance testing.

Insights:
- Extreme Greed produced the strongest profitability.
- Fear regimes showed different risk-taking behavior.
- High-frequency traders outperformed low-frequency traders.
- Behavioral clusters reveal exploitable trading archetypes.

Recommendations:
1. Increase risk allocation during Greed periods.
2. Reduce position size during Extreme Fear.
3. Track high-performing trader cohorts.
4. Use sentiment as a portfolio risk overlay.
